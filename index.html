Hannah Wang

Welcome to my homepage & portfolio!

I am currently a senior studying Astronomy at Cornell University, with minors in Business and Creative Writing. I mainly write poetry and non-fiction prose, but have been exploring creative fiction as well.

At college, I write and edit for Cornell Claritas and have recently been writing for SOLA Network's online publication through their Young Writer's Cohort program. In my free time, I also enjoy experimenting with poetry and writing blog articles.

When I'm not writing, you might find me stargazing, dancing, or spending time with friends (usually with food involved)!
